Cleaning and Tidying data

Our goal is to merge both data frame and clean this data frame. This is the process we are going to take:

  1. Merge the two data frame.
  2. Clean data.
  3. Make additional columns for ease of analysis.
  4. Observe for outliers and filter them out if needed. (This step will be proceeded along the analysis phase).

Checking for null values.

dt %>%
